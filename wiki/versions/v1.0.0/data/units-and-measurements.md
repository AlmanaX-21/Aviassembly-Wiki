# Units & Measurements

Reference for the units used in the game's internal simulation.

## Physics
*   **Distance**: Unity Units (Meters).
*   **Mass**: Arbitrary Mass Units (AMU).
*   **Time**: Seconds.
*   **Velocity**: Meters per Second (m/s).
*   **Force**: Newtons (N).

## Resources
*   **Fuel**: Liters (inferred from volume context).
*   **Electricity**: Ampere-hours (Ah) or generic Energy Units.

## Coordinates
*   **World Origin**: `(0, 0, 0)` is the center of the generated map.
*   **Up**: `+Y`.
*   **Forward**: `+Z`.
