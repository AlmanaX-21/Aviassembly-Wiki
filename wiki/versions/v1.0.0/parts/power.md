# Power Systems

Parts related to electricity generation and storage.

## Battery
Stores electrical energy for electric engines and systems.
*   **Capacity**: Maximum units of electricity stored.

## Solar Panel
Generates electricity from sunlight.
*   **Recharge Speed**: Rate of electricity generation.

## Air Turbine
Generates limited electricity from airflow.
*   **Capacity**: Maximum output/buffer.
*   **Rotor**: Visual component that spins with airflow.

Used to manage heat (mechanics TBD).
