# Getting Started with Aviassembly

Aviassembly is a physics-based aircraft building and flying simulator.

## Core Concepts
1.  **Build**: Construct aircraft using **Parts** (Fuselage, Wings, Engines).
2.  **Balance**: Ensure your Center of Lift (CoL) is behind your Center of Mass (CoM) for stability.
3.  **Fly**: Complete **Missions** or explore the map.
4.  **Manage**: Watch your Fuel and Electricity levels.

## Controls
*   **Pitch**: W/S
*   **Roll**: A/D
*   **Yaw**: Q/E
*   **Throttle**: Shift/Ctrl
*   **Landing Gear**: G
*   **Brakes**: B

## Game Modes
*   **Sandbox**: Unlimited resources, all parts unlocked.
*   **Campaign**: Complete missions to earn Money/Research Points and unlock parts.
